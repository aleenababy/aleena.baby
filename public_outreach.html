Astronomy on Tap

Astronomy on Tap is a group of scientists, educators, writers, and artists reveal how they explore the Universe, at a bar near you! We talk about begning of the Universe to the galaxies, stars and the intricate structure we see today. You can meet many of the bright minds who are behind the amazing discoveries. We also talk about the developments in the astronomical instrumentation, by which we could observe and confirm many of our theories. These presenters are usally from the local instituions nearby. However, we will bring some people from around the world so that you can interact with them.
Along with some of other collegues from University of Cologne, we founded a chapter of Astronomy on Tap on April 2020. Through this chapter we are explaining the complex astronomical theories to the public. Follow us here:
