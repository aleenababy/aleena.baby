My journey

Story of a dreamer

"Somewhere something incredible is waiting to be known" - Carl Sagan

In my 7th class, our class teacher asked us to create a write up about what you wanna become when you grow up. Out of nowhere, this quote popped up in mind and I wrote that I wanted to become an astronomer. Not only I just wrote but also I dreamed of observing through a big telescope and riding a spaceship. Sadly, right before I put my leg on that unknown planet for the first time my alarm wakes me up every time.

But I never stopped that dream. After doing my preliminary school in AUP school, Vazhavatta, Wayanad and GHSS Kakkavayal, Wayanad, I did my Bachelor's (WMO Arts and Science College, Wayanad, Kerala, India) and Master's(Gandhigram Rural Institute- Deemed University, Tamil Nadu, India) in Physics. I did multiple projects and summer/ winter schools in various Institutions in India and abroad. The sad part is I will not be piloting a starship anytime soon. But I also learned that the universe is strange, wonderful and vast to be explored by a starship. So I turned my attention into astronomy, using a telescope. Now I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Cologne, Germany. Looking the vast world through a telescope from my living room.
