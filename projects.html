Modeling of diffusion-advection flows in Photodissociation regions


Molecular clouds are the birth place of stars in the Galaxy When a star forms it will irradiate the clouds nearby. Physics and chemistry of this regions will be influenced by the radiation from the star. I am studying how the transport of molecules influence the chemistry and physics of the medium.

Pulsars

In this project, I developed a model to understand the distribution of Pulsars in the galaxy. This project aimed to observe the regions where a fast radio burst (FRB) could happen. In order to identify the regions, we created a model of the distribution of pulsars in the Milky way and compared it with the currently observed distribution of pulsars. This project involves developing computer codes (Python) to simulate distributions, analyzing the graphical outputs, and using the results to learn about the distribution of pulsars in the Milky Way at any time. In addition, I performed the statistical analysis, compared the data with the current pulsar population, and developed methods to detect and remove outliers.
Guide: Prof. Avinash Deshpande, Raman Research Institute, Bengaluru, India
